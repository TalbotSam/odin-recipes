Bagel

This is a breakfast I've been having lately. It's yum and surprisingly full of protein.

Bea put me onto this breakfast and honestly it's a great way to start the day.

Ingredients

  • A bagel
  • Peanut butter
  • A toaster

Method

  1. Cut bagel in half.
  2. Put bagel in toaster and go for about a 3 or 4 on the toaster. Obviously different toasters change but whatever.
  3. Slap some peanutbutter on that sucker

bon appetit!
