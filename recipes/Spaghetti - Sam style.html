Ingredients

Method

  1. Get some onions going
  2. Chuck the veg and mince into the pan
  3. tomatoes and everything else including seasoning into the pan
  4. Think about starting the pasta
  5. Start the pasta soonish or now
  6. When the pasta is done and you're hungry add pasta to the pan stuff
  7. Look up a recipe with more effort put into it if it doesn't look edible by this stage or you think something has gone wrong
